# German Learning Curriculum - Level B2

Curriculum based on CEFR guidelines and Goethe-Institut for B2 level (Selbstständige Sprachverwendung / Independent User).

## B2 Level Description

A person at B2 level:
- Understands the main ideas of complex text on both concrete and abstract topics
- Can engage in discussions on specialized topics in their field
- Communicates fluently and spontaneously with native speakers
- Can formulate clear, detailed statements on various topics
- Expresses their position and argues for and against

---

### Speaking

### Reading
- Press articles and commentaries

- Instructions and regulations
- Specialized texts from one's field

### Writing
- Formal letters (complaints, applications, grievances)
- Argumentative essays
- Reports and summaries
- Summaries of complex texts
- Detailed descriptions and narratives

## B2 Grammar

### Konjunktiv II
- Wishes and dreams (Ich wünschte, ich hätte...)
- Hypothetical situations (Wenn ich reich wäre...)
- Polite requests (Könnten Sie mir helfen?)
- Advice (An deiner Stelle würde ich...)
- Konjunktiv II Past (Wenn ich das gewusst hätte...)

### Passive Voice (Passiv)
- Passive in all tenses
- Passive with modal verbs
- Zustandspassiv vs Vorgangspassiv
- Impersonal passive (Es wird getanzt)
- Alternatives to passive

### Relative Clauses (Relativsätze)
- Relative pronouns in genitive (dessen, deren)
- Relative clauses with prepositions
- Was as relative pronoun
- Wo, wohin, woher in relative clauses

### Reported Speech (Indirekte Rede)
- Konjunktiv I in reported speech
- Tense shifting
- Dependent questions
- Commands in reported speech

### Complex Conjunctions
- Concession: obwohl, obgleich, trotzdem
- Result: sodass, so...dass
- Time: während, bevor, nachdem, sobald
- Condition: falls, sofern, es sei denn
- Purpose: damit, um...zu

### Nominalization
- Creating nouns from verbs
- Nouns from adjectives
- Use in formal style

### Participles as Adjectives
- Partizip I as adjective (der schlafende Hund)
- Partizip II as adjective (das geöffnete Fenster)
- Extended attributes

### Prepositions with Genitive
- wegen, trotz, während, statt
- innerhalb, außerhalb, oberhalb, unterhalb
- aufgrund, infolge, anlässlich

## Communication Situations

### Product/Service Complaint
Describing the problem, requesting a solution, negotiating compensation, escalating the matter, written correspondence.

Describing symptoms and medical history, understanding diagnosis, asking about treatment, scheduling tests, discussing medication.

Introducing yourself, describing experience, strengths and weaknesses, salary expectations, questions for the employer.

### Discussion About Current Events
Expressing opinions about news, argumentation, responding to different views, finding compromise.

### Expressing and Defending a Position
Formulating a thesis, providing arguments, refuting counterarguments, summarizing.

### Conflict Resolution
Describing the problem, expressing emotions, listening to the other side, finding solutions, apologizing.

### Negotiations
Presenting expectations, argumentation, concessions, reaching compromise, finalizing agreements.

### Filing Complaints
Describing the situation, expressing dissatisfaction, demanding action, escalation, written form.

### Scheduling Meetings
Proposing a time, changing plans, confirmations, canceling, reminders.

### Small Talk
Safe topics, polite questions, maintaining conversation, ending conversation.
